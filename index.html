Understanding the Mood Scale
Each day you'll rate your highest and lowest mood point on a -3 to +3 scale.
What each level means
+3Elevated / unusually energized
+2Good day, performing well
+1Slightly above baseline
0Normal / neutral baseline
-1Slightly off, manageable
-2Struggling, affecting performance
-3Significantly impaired

Add to Your Phone
If you haven't already — on iPhone: tap the Share button in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen". On Android: tap the browser menu, then "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen".
📅 Daily Reminder
Downloads a .ics file — open it to add a repeating daily reminder to your calendar app.
Export Data
CSV is raw data for spreadsheets or your doctor. PDF is a formatted clinical report ready to share.
Share with Care Team
Creates a read-only link with your recent data encoded inside it. Give the 4-digit PIN to whoever you share it with — they'll need both the link and the PIN to view anything.
About This App
AthleteTrack stores all data locally on this device. Nothing is sent to any server. Exporting and sharing is entirely under your control. For medical decisions, always work with your licensed care team.
